Maven 2

Configure a dependency in your pom for ${pom.artifactId} and add the Silver Mind Software release and snapshot repos.

<dependencies>
...
    <dependency>
        <groupId>com.silvermindsoftware.stripes</groupId>
        <artifactId>stripes-guice</artifactId>
        <version>1.5.2</version>
        <scope>compile</scope>
    </dependency>
...
</dependencies>
